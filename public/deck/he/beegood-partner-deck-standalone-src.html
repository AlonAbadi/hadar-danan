10 / 14 · BUSINESS MODEL

Three revenue lines.

01 · Establishment

Strategy & platform build.

  • One-time fee for onboarding a cultural leader into the atelier
  • Includes positioning strategy, narrative architecture, and full multi-tenant platform build on beegood.online
  • The core of the engagement — and the primary revenue event
02 · Ongoing

Commission on platform revenue.

  • Monthly commission on gross revenue through the client's platform
  • Client operates their own payment terminal; beegood invoices commission
  • Recurring, aligned upside as the client scales
03 · Production

Member-rate content days.

  • Atelier members receive discounted video production through beegood studio
  • Priced per shoot day, not per deliverable
  • Keeps craft close; optional, not a dependency
Establishment funds the craft. Commission compounds the scale. Production keeps the signal alive.
